I'm Yanyi

a designer(yes), a technologist(maybe), an artist(???)
I wander in the planet, collecting puzzles and humbly putting a piece of mine.
That's the journey I go, with the flow of heart and mind.

I was born and raised in China. I studied and worked across Europe. I live in Germany for some culture & nature inspiration now.

I’m obsessed on uncovering pattern, through reading, observing, experiencing and making. I'm fascinated in blending art and science for mess & fun. I'd like to co-create with people from non-(ux)-design background. Previously, I worked with people from computer science, biology, business, theater and anthropology (so much fun and cool stuff). Recently, I'm pacing slowly on the design road, and try to take my brain out of UX zone to better link with other disciplines. I'm learning how to scale design (and designer) in bigger complex system.

This website is a silent corner where I post some non-NDA fun works which reflect who I am as a designer in the passing time.

Due to NDA, please contact me via email (yanyicontact at gmail dot com) for CV and the case studies of commercial projects for clients.
